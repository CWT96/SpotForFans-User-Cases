# SpotForFans — Interactive User Cases

**SpotForFans.com** is a nationality-tagged venue discovery platform built for the 2026 FIFA World Cup across all 11 U.S. host cities. It helps international fans find bars and venues where their own national fan base gathers — and helps venues capture compounding fan traffic through a snowball visibility model.

## What Is SpotForFans?

On Yelp or Google, every bar has the same odds of being discovered — you're one of hundreds. On SpotForFans, when a handful of Brazilian fans mark a venue as their spot, other Brazilian fans see that signal and follow. Visibility compounds. The more fans pick a venue, the more new fans find it.

**Static odds vs. dynamic odds.** That's the difference.

### For Fans
Find your tribe in an unfamiliar city. See exactly which venues are gathering spots for your national fan base — not just "sports bars," but restaurants, beer gardens, nightclubs, and watch parties where your people are going.

### For Venues
The World Cup guarantees fans in the city. It doesn't guarantee fans in your bar. SpotForFans is how you make sure they find you — and how early-listed venues build a compounding visibility advantage before tournament traffic arrives.
